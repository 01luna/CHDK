IXUS220HS / ELPH300HS Notes

The default <ALT> button is PLAY. It can be assigned to VIDEO in CHDK settings.

Long Press the <ALT> button to access the original function.

Booting from FAT32 is supported, so multipartition cards are not required or supported.

This camera has an ND filter and does not have an iris.

Known bugs:
The camera crashes when PTP is connected. There is no known workaround.

For more information please refer to IXUS200HS/ELPH300HS Porting Thread:
http://chdk.setepontos.com/index.php?topic=6341
