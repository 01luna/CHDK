************************************
IXUS155/ELPH150 Notes
************************************

To enter CHDK <ALT> mode use the Playback [>] button.

Playback [>] or Help [?] button can be configured as <ALT> mode button.

KNOWN BUGS
**********
- No known bugs, but mostly untested port.
