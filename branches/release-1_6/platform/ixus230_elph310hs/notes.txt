IXUS230HS / ELPH310HS Notes

To enter CHDK <Alt> mode use the Playback [>] button.

Short Press of Playback button will toggle <ALT> mode.
Long Press of Playback button will execute original Canon functionality.

Support for dual partition is not available.

This camera has and ND filter and does not have an adjustable aperture.

For more information please refer to IXUS230HS/ELPH310HS Porting Thread:
http://chdk.setepontos.com/index.php?topic=7149.30

This camera has very little free RAM in the Canon heap. EXMEM is enabled
to provide sufficient memory for CHDK.
